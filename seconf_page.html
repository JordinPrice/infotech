My Non-Linear Post-Grad Timeline

2022 2023 2024
Earned my bachelors, graduating from Ithaca College. I had no set plans after graduation which left me feeling lost for awhile. Two months later I started teaching history at a charter school in the Bronx. Quit within seven months, and was back to square one. I finally figured out the field I saw myself in long-term and started my graduate school application process. I only applied to three schools: Pratt, NYU, and Parsons. I still had no plan but I remained hopeful that eveything would work itself out. After being unemployed for nine months, I finally landed a job and shortly recevied my acceptance from Pratt. Now, I am currently in my first year at Pratt, enjoying every bit of it. I am reminded everyday that I chose the right school. Although this is just my first semester, I already feel like I've learned so much. Excited to see how 2024 ends.
Thank you for scrolling through and learning a bit about me!
